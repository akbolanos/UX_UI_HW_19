My UX Skills

Wireframing

I love the early stages of ideation and design. It is a paramount to the begin of a project, and can quickly showcase what it is you are trying to do. I enjoy the many stages of wireframing, from pen and paper to hi-fi wireframes. Each time you complete a wireframe you discover something new.

Information Architecture

I enjoy taking the time to organize information in a tangible way. I believe the structural design of information should be accessible and easy to maneuver. Good information architecture helps the user to understand where they are, where the information they want is.
